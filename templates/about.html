OUR STORY

A New Beginning After Retirement

My name is Muhammad Saleem — founder of ApnaGhar. I spent my professional career as an Electrical & Mechanical Supervisor. After retirement, I decided to put my skills and experience to good use.

I started with a document services shop in Akhtar Colony, Karachi — because I have strong MS Word skills and experience in creating professional documents. Soon, I expanded to include property rental and sale services as well.

ApnaGhar is built on one simple promise — honest, reliable, and affordable service for the people of Karachi. Whether you need a rent agreement, a property listing, or just a CV — we are here to help.
