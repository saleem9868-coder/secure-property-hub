Mutation / Intiqal — Kya Hota Hai?

📖 Mutation ki Taareef

Mutation (Intiqal اردو میں) ek qanooni amal hai jis mein government ki land records mein property ki milkiyat tabdeel ki jaati hai. Yeh tab zaroor hota hai jab property bechni, wiraasat, ya gift di jaaye.

Simple alfaaz mein: Jab aap ghar kharidte hain — sarkari records mein naam aapka hona chahiye — yahi Mutation hai!
